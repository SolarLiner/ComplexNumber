Enumerator
TRIGONOMETRIC 

Trigonometric form (r*e^{i*theta*pi}).

CARTESIAN 

Cartesian form (a+bi).

COMPONENT 

Component form ([r, theta]).

PHASOR 

Prints complex number as a sum of sin and cos functions (r*(cos(theta)+ i*sin(theta))).
